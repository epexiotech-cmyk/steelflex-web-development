National Logistics Hub

Client: Global Logistics Ltd. Location: Delhi NCR Completion: 2024
The Challenge

Scale meets Speed

The client required a massive 500,000 sq. ft. warehousing facility operational within a critical 8-month window. The primary structural constraint was the need for 30-meter clear spans to allow unobstructed automated vehicle movement, while also supporting a heavy rooftop solar array.

Our Approach

Engineered for Efficiency

  • Dual-Site Fabrication: To meet the ambitious timeline, we split heavy fabrication across two facilities, running 24/7.
  • Custom Truss Design: Economized steel usage by 15% using a bespoke truss system for the 30m spans.
  • Modular Erection: Simultaneous erection of multiple bays reduced onsite time by 4 weeks.
The Outcome

Delivered Ahead of Schedule

The National Logistics Hub is now a fully operational node, processing over 10,000 parcels daily. The project stands as a benchmark for rapid industrial infrastructure delivery.
