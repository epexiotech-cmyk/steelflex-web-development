Infrastructure & Technology

Our manufacturing facility in Vadodara is equipped with advanced automated machinery to ensure precision fabrication and high-volume output. With a production capacity of over 16,000 MT per year, our infrastructure features fully automated H-Beam welding lines, high-precision CNC laser and plasma cutting systems, and heavy-duty forming equipment. This state-of-the-art technology allows us to deliver international quality standards with zero-error fabrication, meeting the demands of complex industrial and commercial projects.
