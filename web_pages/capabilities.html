Operational Capabilities & Execution

From Design to Delivered Reality: A Systematic Approach to Heavy Engineering.

Core Competencies

  • Design & Detailing: Advanced 3D modeling and structural analysis using Tekla and STAAD.Pro for zero-error fabrication.
  • Heavy Fabrication: Production capacity of 500+ MT per month for complex industrial frameworks.
  • On-Site Erection: Specialized teams for safe and rapid assembly of structural steel in challenging environments.
  • Turnkey Project Management: End-to-end oversight ensuring timeline adherence and seamless integration of all project phases.

Our Process

  1. 1. Consultation & Design

    Understanding client requirements and developing optimized structural designs.

  2. 2. Engineering & Detailing

    Creating precise fabrication drawings and bill of materials (BOM).

  3. 3. Procurement & Planning

    Sourcing high-grade raw materials and scheduling production milestones.

  4. 4. Fabrication & Quality Control

    Manufacturing components with rigorous stage-wise inspection.

  5. 5. Logistics & Erection

    Safe transport and expert on-site assembly for final handover.

Quality & Compliance

At SteelFlex, quality is non-negotiable. Our operations adhere to global ISO 9001:2015 standards. Every beam, column, and truss undergoes ultrasonic testing (UT) and magnetic particle inspection (MPI) to identify and rectify internal flaws. We are committed to zero-accident workplaces, enforcing strict HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) protocols on every site.
