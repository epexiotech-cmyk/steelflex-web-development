National Logistics Hub

Client: Global Logistics Ltd. Location: Delhi NCR Year: 2024 Status: Completed

The Challenge

The client required a massive 500,000 sq. ft. warehousing facility to be operational within a tight 8-month deadline. Key structural demands included 30-meter clear spans to allow unobstructed forklift movement and a roof structure capable of supporting heavy solar panel installations.

Scope of Work

  • Structural Design and Analysis for high wind load zones.
  • Fabrication of 2,500 MT of primary and secondary steel members.
  • Supply and installation of standing seam roofing system.
  • Civil foundation coordination and anchor bolt fixing.

Our Solution

We deployed a pre-engineered building (PEB) solution using high-strength Grade 350 steel. A truss-frame design was chosen to achieve the 30m span economically. To meet the deadline, fabrication was split across two of our facilities, running 24/7 shifts. On-site, we used a modular erection strategy, allowing different bays to be assembled simultaneously.

Key Project Highlights

Speed

Project delivered 15 days ahead of schedule.

Innovation

Designed a custom gutter system to handle peak monsoon rainfall.

Safety

100,000 safe man-hours with zero lost-time injuries.

The Result

The National Logistics Hub is now a fully operational nodal center, processing over 10,000 parcels daily. The robust structural design has provided the client with a maintenance-free asset that accommodates their current needs and future expansion plans.
